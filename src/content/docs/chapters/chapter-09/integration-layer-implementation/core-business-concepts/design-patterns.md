The Hub is the foundational entity in Data Vault modeling, representing the Core Business Concept (CBC): the unique business keys that identify entities in the business domain, such as customers, products, or orders. Its design rationale and structure are documented in the [Design Pattern - Data Vault - Hub](/patterns/design-patterns/design-pattern-data-vault-hub/).
