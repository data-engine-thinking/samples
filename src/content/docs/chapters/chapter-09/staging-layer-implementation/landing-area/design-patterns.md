The Landing Area is the first point of arrival for data entering the data solution, where the source data delta is captured before it is processed downstream. Its design rationale and structure are documented in the [Design Pattern - Generic - Landing Area](/patterns/design-patterns/design-pattern-generic-landing-area/).
